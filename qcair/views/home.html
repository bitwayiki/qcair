Understanding

Its easy to feel overwhelmed or out of place when entering to a new country. We have many resources to help immigrants and refugees understand the culture of the United States. We also give opprotunity to our voulunteers to connect with people from different countries.

Housing & Jobs

We help those attempting to settle in to find housing. Moving is hard enough -moving to another country is even harder. Through our programs we provide the possibility of an easier transition.

Education

We are confident in our ability to provide an avenue of study and/or education for those who are trying to adjust to living in the United States. This is not limited to highschool or college students.We cover a wide variety of education from language learning, to art (dancing, painting, sculpting, etc.) and more!

A Letter from our President

Welcome to Quad Cities Alliance for Immigrants and Refugees (QCAIR)! In this day and age of fear of immigrants and refugees, we work to facilitate understanding of all cultures and peoples. Our mission statement is: “Assist Immigrants and Refugees intergrate into the Quad City community by advocating for immigrants and refugees and collaborating with appropriate service providers. We do this by first applying the principles of Asset Based Community Development (ABCD) by taking advantage of existing services provided by partner or community agencies. When we find a “gap” in services we work to fill that gap. Historically we have worked on housing, education efforts, assisting immigrant and refugess in obtaining a job, teaching about health issues and creating a friendly environment with our Positive interactions with Police (PIP) team. One of our new efforts, in conjunction with our partners is creating an “Honoring Us Citizenship” dinner. These dinners will honor those immigrant and refugees who have chosen to become US Citizens. If you are looking for a way to learn about immigrants and refugees or are interested in helping them tor learn the skills needed to become US citizens or members of our community, or providing assistance to immigrant and refugees, we invite you to fill out a volunteer form and submit it. Our structure includes a Board of Directors, a Board of Advisors, and Education Team and a Development Team. We are seeking individuals who are wanting to assist these groups as well as action teams under the Education and Development teams. Finally, we are always looking for donations to support our operations and projects. If you are interested in making a donation please click on the donate button, or send a contribution to QCAIR, 1800 3rd Ave, Rock Island Il 61201. I look forward to developing both QCAIR and our relationship as we go forward.
-Grant Curtis,
President
